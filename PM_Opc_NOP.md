\== NOP = No Operation ==

| Hex | Mnemonic | Cycles |
| --- | -------- | ------ |
| FF  | NOP      | 8      |

### Execute

Nothing

### Description

Does nothing, used for short delays.

### Conditions

None

### Examples

`; Delay 8 Cycles`
**`NOP`**
